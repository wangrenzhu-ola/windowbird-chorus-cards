# App Store Metadata Draft

## Name
WindowBird Chorus Cards

## Subtitle
Private window birdsong cards

## Promotional Text
Turn a two-minute window listen into a private card with rhythm, direction, weather, mood, and an optional window view photo.

## Description
WindowBird Chorus Cards helps beginner bird listeners build a gentle sound map without pressure to identify species. Open Morning Chorus, choose the sound shape you heard, then add direction, weather, mood, an optional window view photo, and a private note on the listen card screen. Review saved listens as neighborhood sound dots and earn quiet exploration badges.

You start with 100 Chorus Credits — enough for ten new listen cards. Each additional new save costs 10 credits; editing existing cards is free. Buy more credits in the Shop tab when you need them. Consumable credits do not restore across reinstalls.

Listen cards, window view photos, and notes stay on this device. The listening flow does not require microphone recording. Limited tracking may be requested only to understand whether credit offers feel helpful.

## Keywords
birds, nature, backyard, journal, sound, morning, map, mindfulness

## Privacy Summary
Listen cards, window view photos, and notes stay on this device. The app declares photo library, camera, microphone, and tracking permission descriptions for window view photos, optional birdsong listening helpers, and offer measurement; the core listening flow does not prompt for microphone access. The app has no login and no backend service for this version. Privacy Policy and User Agreement open in an in-app WebView.
